Welcome

Nature × Matter explores how biological principles can inspire new materials that respond, regenerate, and sustain life. Led by Hamideh Rezvani Alanagh, our work bridges chemistry, soft matter physics, and sustainability-driven biomaterials design.

We believe science should not only explain the world, but help build it in better, more sustainable ways. Our interdisciplinary approach combines chemistry, materials science, and biophysics to design responsive materials for health, agriculture, and environmental solutions.
