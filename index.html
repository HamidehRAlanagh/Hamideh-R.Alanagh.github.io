Welcome to Nature × Matter

Exploring nature-inspired materials to address challenges in health, sustainability, and the environment.

Our Mission

Nature × Matter bridges chemistry, biology, and materials science to design dynamic, sustainable solutions. Led by Hamideh Rezvani Alanagh, our work focuses on creating materials that respond, adapt, and support life in meaningful ways.

We study biomolecular assemblies, engineer responsive scaffolds, and apply advanced characterization to understand and control soft matter systems. From therapeutics and diagnostics to sustainable agriculture and water treatment, we believe that learning from nature enables us to build a better future.
