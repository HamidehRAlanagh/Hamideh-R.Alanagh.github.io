Welcome

Nature × Matter explores how biological principles can inspire new materials that respond, regenerate, and sustain life. Led by Hamideh Rezvani Alanagh, our work bridges chemistry, soft matter physics, and sustainability-driven biomaterials design.

Research

Our projects include protein coacervates, biomimetic interface design, and dynamic scaffolds for environmental and biomedical applications.
